Instructions
Move with W A S D or the touch pad.
Press J to punch.
Press K to use special power.
Press E to use domain.
Press 1 for Gojo, 2 for Toji, 3 for Maki, 4 for Yuji, 5 for Todo, 6 for Hakari.

When domain is active, everyone gives 2x damage.
During domain, every character gets a white aura.
Yuji gives 2x punch damage, but he also takes 2x damage.
Todo gives 2x damage.
Hakari gives 2x damage and takes much less damage from enemies.
Every round has one health box.
The health box gives back some HP, not full HP.
